Earth Science

By DataHub Team • 45 lessons • 20 datasets

Explore Earth's systems through the lens of data science. Students will analyze real-world datasets to understand weather patterns, climate change, geological processes, and environmental science while building essential data literacy skills. Each lesson aligns with NGSS standards and provides scaffolded data science instruction throughout the academic year.
